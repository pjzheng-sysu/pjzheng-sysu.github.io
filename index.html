Biography

Associate Professor, Ph.D. Supervisor

Deputy Director, Institute of Cyberspace Security, School of Computer Science, Sun Yat-sen University

Deputy Director, Key Laboratory of Information Technology, Ministry of Education

IEEE Member; Member of the China Computer Federation (CCF); Senior Member of the China Society of Image and Graphics; Committee Member of the Digital Media Forensics and Security Technical Committee.

I have published over 40 research papers, including 12 papers as first or corresponding author in top-tier venues in information security (Top-4), IEEE/ACM Transactions, and CCF Class-A conferences. I hold nearly 20 granted national invention patents. I have led two projects funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC), one sub-project under the National Key R&D Program, four projects funded by the Guangdong Natural Science Foundation, and six other vertical research projects. Additionally, I have played a key role in nearly 10 major national and provincial-level projects.

Graduated students have joined leading IT companies, such as Tencent, Alibaba, ByteDance, Huawei, and NetEase Games.
